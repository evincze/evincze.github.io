About Me Resume Projects

The Playtester - Binary Bandits

This was a game that I worked on called "The Playtester" that was created for the 2024 TO Jam. Me and 2 other programmers completed this game in 2.5 days. My duties during the development of the game include the player movement and input, creating UI menus such as a pause menu and a settings menu. I also created the audio system for voice lines and sound effects.

RPG Project - George Brown College

This is a school project that I worked on in my second year of college where I was tasked with creating an RPG game. I worked on this project completely by myself, I created an audio system, battle system, dialogue system, etc. I also worked on the player and NPC movement, main menu screen, settings screen and also the pause screen. For the battle system I have a feature similar to Earthbound where depending on how the battle starts, either the player or the enemy will have advantage in battle with a first move. In my battle system there is also elemental damage depending on the attack type the player uses and the enemy's element type.

Cells At War (Nucleus Minigame) - George Brown College / McMasters University

This is a minigame that I worked on during my third year at George Brown College. I was asked if I wanted to participate in creating a game for Biology Students at McMaster University. I was giving the task to program a system to generate certain nucleotides as the game progresses. I programmed a way to move the groups of DNA across the screen to wait for the players input and created a way to undo the last move and retry.

Minecraft Crafting System - George Brown College

This is a project that I worked on for one of my classes at George Brown. I was given the task to create a crafting system similar to Minecraft.
